About me

I am a master's graduate in mathematics from Central University of Kashmir, with a thesis on Ranks and Presentations for Transformation Semigroups. My research interests lie in algebra (commutative algebra, representation theory, semigroup theory), number theory (algebraic number theory), and topology (algebraic topology), with a strong focus on algebraic geometry and Galois theory.
